Delivery time

 Average delivery time ranges from 45-55 minutes.
Delivery times vary based on traffic,
the restaurant's ability
to prepare food and weather conditions.
We recommend that large orders
be placed at least two hours in advance,
but if you require a specific lunch time delivery,
24 hour notice is recommended.
